Life outside Earth?

01 | Technology

With an estimated two trillion galaxies in the universe, it’s easy to speculate that we’re not the only intelligent species to exist in all of space. Recent headlines about the US shooting down unidentified objects only add to the speculation. Plus, the discovery of a potentially habitable super-Earth also gives credence to this concept.
